About Nova Lion Tech

Nova Lion Tech is a small creative startup focused on crafting digital tools that make UX research simpler, faster, and more inclusive. Our team designs lightweight web applications that help students, designers, and small teams run usability studies with confidence.

Our motto — Courage in every click — represents the spirit of exploration and innovation. Whether you’re analyzing user feedback or preparing your next design sprint, Nova Lion Tech gives you the courage to test, learn, and grow.

Our Focus

  • Accessible, mobile-friendly design tools
  • Ready-to-use UX testing templates
  • Inclusive experiences built with accessibility first
  • Simple web utilities that encourage creativity
