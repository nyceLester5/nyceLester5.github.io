About Nova Lion

We’re a tiny studio focused on practical UX helpers. Our tools are intentionally small, fast, and accessible, so students and lean teams can run better studies without wrestling complex software.

What we build

  • Task timer + SEQ capture with export
  • Consent & note templates for consistent studies
  • Auto-formatted summaries
  • Keyboard-first UI, ARIA-labeled controls

Why we build it

Great research doesn’t need heavy tools. We ship focused web apps students can trust in class and teams can trust in sprint. If it isn’t simple and accessible, it isn’t Nova Lion.
